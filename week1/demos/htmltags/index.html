Div containers

Divs are containers that can hold other elements, including other Divs.




Links

This is a Div inside of a Link. Notice how the area you can click on extends to the right, beyond the text.

Links can send you to other websites.
Links can even force you to open a new tab when you click them.
Links can open local files. These can be anything, like images, documents (such as PDFs), or even other HTML files.
If a link sends you to local folder without specifying a specific file in that folder, it will default to opening the “index.html” files within that folder.

Links send you to other elements on the same page too. These are called anchor links, but we’ll go over them in another demo.

Images

HTML supports many image types, including JPGs, PNGs, and even SVGs (vector files).

Images can be embedded from a variety of sources.

Images can be linked from online files.

Images can be linked from local files in your site’s repository.

You can even organize images into subfolders in your site’s repository.
